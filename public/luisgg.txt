Luis.GG is a web site made for players to analyse and share theirs statistics with each other. This service allows each user to search through their match history, select the match they are interested in and start looking at their game stats. This site also shows the achievements each player got in that specific game. These achievements include badges such as Top Farm, Top Damage, Most CC Dealt, etc... This with the purpose of players sharing their achievements with each other. APIs used are: Match, Account, Summoner and Champion-Mastery.
